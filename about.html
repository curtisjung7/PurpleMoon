Purple Moon

aka Curtis SeungYoung Jung

Purple Moon is the artist based in LA. He borned in South Korea, and he moved to Chicago when he was 18 for college.

After 3 years, he moved to LA when he was 21. Now he lives in LA and does his musical works based on there.

Education

Hankuk Academy of Foreign Studies, 2011 - 2014

Columbia College Chicago, BFA Contemporary Urban and Popular Music Major 2014 - 2016

California Institute of the Arts, BFA Musical Arts/Experimental Pop Major 2017 - current
